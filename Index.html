I'm a storyteller and web developer from Anambra State, Nigeria. I blend gripping narratives with clean, immersive web experiences — writing worlds people get lost in, then building the digital stages that bring them to life.

Whether I'm plotting a crime dynasty's downfall, tracing a love story born on TikTok, or surviving a zombie apocalypse, every story I write is built on raw emotion, real tension, and characters that stay with you.
